Statement

Posted by   2015-03-08

Some of my earliest memories are of making art. My father was an artist so my creative endeavors were always supported and encouraged. I am intrigued by the tossed words, phrases, and moments in time of everyday life that are simple but rich in their simplicity. I enjoy creating images that portray these many levels of meaning. My art is a reflection of my past and present experiences with people, places and things.
